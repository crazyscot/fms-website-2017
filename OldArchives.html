Results forThe First Under 23 National Championships arehere Foil and Epee

The inaugural NZU23 Champs took place in Christchurch on Sunday 6 July.   The numbers may have been small but the intensity on the pistes was high.  Thanks to all the competitors and the referees, and to everyone who helped with organisation and running the event, including the canteen, on the day.

Congratulations to the new National Champions, both from Fencing North –
Mixed Epee: Jurgens van Zyl from North Shore Fencing Club;  
Mixed Foil:  Hamish Chan from the NZ Academy of Fencing.   

NZU23 Champs Fair Play Award for Mixed Epee :  Isaac Officer, The Fencing Institute/Fencing Mid South

NZU23 Champs Fair Play Award for Mixed Foil:  Hamish Chan, NZ Academy of Fencing, Fencing North.

In the teams matches, NZU23 Epee A (Jonathan Brill, Chris Knowles, Johann Ryu) beat NZU23 Epee B (Isaac Officer, Laura Bythell, Sam Townshend) 45/29.

Team Mainland Epee (Liam James, Mark Rance, Andreas Sesun) narrowly beat Team NZU23 Epee (Laura Bythell, Jurgens van Zyl, Johann Ryu) 45/38.

Thanks

Kate
