UNITED

fencingforus@gmail.com Uniting UFC, CFA and FOCUS

     

Lessons start March 3rd.
We will meet at Shirley Intermediate School Hall at 7.00 pm on Tuesdays. Enter the School off North Parade. The Hall is to the left of the Admin Block and parking is available in the grounds.
Our normal programme includes a warm-up, foot work, move of the day, group and individual lessons and bout fencing. We concentrate on foil for beginners and introduce epee and sabre to more experienced fencers
All our coaches are very experienced as fencers, administrators and coaches, locally and nationally. They volunteer their time and experience so our fees are low.

MEMBERSHIP:  The $130 club fee includes affiliation to the regional and national body for the year and entitles you to take part in events in NZ. Fees are to be paid to the Treasurer by the third week of March. A special family group fee can be arranged.
 Beginner Classes
The introduction to fencing course will run for eight sessions usually starting on the first Tuesday in March at 7 – 8.00pm initially, cost $60. This fee will count towards your club membership if you decide to join for the year. We will gradually extend the time and introduce individual lessons as your knowledge/ interest in fencing grows.
United has been introducing fencers to the sport for more than 50 years. Many of our fencers have won Canterbury and New Zealand senior, U20s and school titles and all our fencers have enjoyed the sport and art of fencing. Parents often learn along with their children. BRING/WEAR:

Track-suit pants or stretch long trousers [no shorts or jeans]

Tee shirt, sweatshirt, gym shoes
A glove for your fencing hand preferably with a gauntlet
An exercise book or flip file for notes

WE WILL SUPPLY THE FOLLOWING:

Foil, mask, padding, training jacket

NOTE: SAFETY ----YOU MUST NEVER FENCE WITHOUT THE CORRECT PROTECTIVE GEAR.
