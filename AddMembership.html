BENEFITS OF MEMBERSHIP:

. Participation in local and nation widen fencing events(social and competitive).
. Opportunity to take part in local, national and international competitions as a fencer,coach, referee or manager.
. Chances for selection to represent Mid-South or New Zealand in the three weapons of sabre, epee or foil and advanced training events.
. Gain a local, island or national ranking.

 

PLEASE NOTE:

This form should be used by clubs to enter the details of those members who they have received affiliation / membership fees from.

We recommend joining through your local club where possible. If you wish to pay your affiliation fees as an individual please use this form and mark your club as none.

Payments can be made direct to the Fencing Mid South Treasurer, Cheques to made out to Fencing Mid South.

Or direct credited with your full name as the reference to Fencing Mid South, Westpac account number 03 0802 0087494 00

Direct credited payments from clubs should reference the name of the club and provide a written list of the members they wish to affiliate with that payment to the Fencing Mid South Treasurer. "
